About Myriam Colella

With over 15 years of experience in healthcare, I bring a wealth of knowledge and compassion to my practice. My journey in caregiving began with a deep desire to help others and make a meaningful difference in people's lives.

Professional Qualifications

  • Registered Nurse with advanced certifications in geriatric care
  • Specialized training in palliative and hospice care
  • Certified in First Aid and CPR
  • Extensive experience with dementia and Alzheimer's care
  • Multilingual: Fluent in English, French, and Italian

My Approach

I believe in treating each client with dignity, respect, and personalized attention. My approach combines professional medical knowledge with a warm, compassionate demeanor that puts clients and their families at ease.

Personal Commitment

Beyond professional qualifications, I bring genuine care and dedication to each relationship. I understand that inviting someone into your home or trusting them with a loved one's care is a significant decision, and I honor that trust with the highest standards of service.
