My Services

Home Care Services

Personal Care Assistance

Help with daily activities including bathing, dressing, grooming, and mobility support to maintain independence and dignity.

Meal Preparation

Nutritionally balanced meal planning and preparation according to dietary needs and preferences.

Medication Management

Assistance with medication reminders and organization to ensure proper dosage and timing.

Specialized Care

Dementia Care

Specialized support for individuals with Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia, focusing on safety and quality of life.

Palliative Care

Compassionate support for individuals with serious illnesses, focusing on pain management and comfort.

Post-Hospitalization Care

Assistance with recovery after hospital discharge, including wound care and rehabilitation support.

Additional Services

Errands & Transportation

Assistance with grocery shopping, pharmacy pickups, and transportation to medical appointments.

Respite Care

Temporary care services to provide family caregivers with much-needed breaks.

Care Coordination

Assistance with scheduling and coordinating medical appointments and other care services.
